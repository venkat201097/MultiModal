MULTI-MODAL LEARNING EXPERIMENT


Instructions:


1) There will be 6 sessions with training and testing phases.

2) In each session, you will learn 10 novel images and their corresponding audio labels. You may be shown 1,2 or 3 samples of an image-audio pair.

3) After training, you will be tested to retrieve the correct image given the audio label, and vice-versa.

4) Image retrieval - Given a test audio sample, click the corresponding image from the given 10.

5) Audio retrieval - Given a test image sample, listen to the 10 given audio files and click the corresponding one.

Please scroll down during testing so that you don't miss any image/audio option.
